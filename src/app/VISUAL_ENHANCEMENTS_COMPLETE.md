## 📊 How to Use

### **Daily Schedule**
1. Click "Show My Schedule" button on dashboard
2. Click "Add Task" to create new scheduled activities
3. Fill in:
   - Task title
   - Category (Study, Exercise, Break, etc.)
   - Start time
   - Duration
   - Priority level
   - Optional reminder
   - Optional notes
4. Or click "AI Schedule" to auto-generate a balanced day plan
5. Filter by time of day (Morning/Afternoon/Evening)
6. Check off tasks as you complete them
7. Track progress in the stats cards

### **New Activities**
1. Click on Art Corner 🎨, Space Explorer 🚀, or Music Time 🎵
2. Choose from 6 fun sub-activities
3. Click "Let's Go!" to start
4. Read fun facts at the bottom!
